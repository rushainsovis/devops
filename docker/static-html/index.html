Tyrannosaurus Rex

The T-Rex was one of the largest land carnivores of all time. Its name means "Tyrant Lizard King".

Triceratops

This three-horned herbivore used its frill and horns for defense against predators like T-Rex.

Velociraptor

Small but swift, the Velociraptor hunted in packs and had a deadly sickle-shaped claw.

Brachiosaurus

This long-necked giant could reach treetops and weighed up to 50 tons!

Stegosaurus

Known for its spiked tail and bony back plates, it used these features for defense and temperature control.

Spinosaurus

One of the largest carnivorous dinosaurs, it had a sail-like fin and was likely a strong swimmer.

Ankylosaurus

Armored like a tank, it had a club-like tail to smash predators. It was a true prehistoric tank.

Pteranodon

Though not a true dinosaur, this flying reptile ruled the skies with a wingspan over 20 feet.

Allosaurus

A fearsome predator before T-Rex, it hunted in groups and had sharp claws and teeth.

Pachycephalosaurus

Famous for its thick, domed skull, used for head-butting rivals during battles.

Parasaurolophus

Known for its long, curved crest, it may have used it to make deep calls across the forest.

Ichthyosaurus

This dolphin-like marine reptile lived in the seas and had large eyes for hunting in the dark.
